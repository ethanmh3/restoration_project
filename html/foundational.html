Foundational Revelation

Restoring What Was Lost

          Gratefully, Heavenly Father is perfectly merciful and wouldn't allow us to continue distancing ourselves from Him forever. He had a plan to restore His true church on the earth once more through a boy from Palmyra, New York name Joseph Smith.

The First Vision

          Joseph Smith himself was confused by all the contention and difference of opinion in the religious world. He decided to seek answers straight from God on the subject and described his experience as follows:

"My object in going to enquire of the Lord was to know which of all the sects was right, that I might know which to join. No sooner therefore did I get possession of myself so as to be able to speak, than I asked the personages who stood above me in the light, which of all the sects was right, (for at this time it had never entered into my heart that all were wrong) and which I should join. I was answered that I must join none of them, for they were all wrong, and the Personage who addressed me said that all their Creeds were an abomination in his sight, that those professors were all corrupt, that “they draw near to me to with their lips but their hearts are far from me, They teach for doctrines the commandments of men, having a form of Godliness but they deny the power thereof. I was informed that I was chosen to be an instrument in the hands of God to bring about some of his purposes in this glorious dispensation.” - Joseph Smith Papers 1838 and 1842 Accounts

The Book of Mormon

          Joseph acted in faith, received revelation straight from God pertaining to the truthfulness of the gospel, and was called to be His prophet to leading the restoration of His church to the earth once more. As part of this restoration, Joseph Smith was directed by a heavenly messenger, Moroni, to uncover an ancient record and translate its contents.

"He said there was a book deposited, written upon gold plates, giving an account of the former inhabitants of this continent, and the source from whence they sprang. He also said that the fulness of the everlasting Gospel was contained in it, as delivered by the Savior to the ancient inhabitants;" - Joseph Smith-History:34

          Joseph translated this ancient record recorded on golden plates through the gift and power of God into what we know today as the Book of Mormon. With the Book of Mormon, the truth of four core gospel doctrines was restored to the earth.

  • Worship of Christ directly through the gospel of Christ from the very beginning
  • 2 Nephi 31
  • Some of the natives of America descend from Israel; they are promised to be brought back to the fold
  • 2 Nephi 30:3-5
  • The latter-day work to build Zion on the American continent
  • 2 Nephi 27:26
  • God speaks to many nations, and they record it
  • 2 Nephi 29:12

Doctrine & Covenants

          The restoration of the Church of Jesus Christ came with an additional book of scripture known as the Doctrine and Covenants. This book contains many of the revelations received by Joseph Smith as well as inspired declarations of truth. Each was given to help establish and govern the kingdom of God on the earth. The Doctrine and Covenants is especially important to the Latter-day Church for its revelations on the temple. The Lord gave His reasons for having this book as follows:

"For they have strayed from mine ordinances, and have broken mine everlasting covenant;

"They seek not the Lord to establish his righteousness, but every man walketh in his own way, and after the image of his own god, whose image is in the likeness of the world, and whose substance is that of an idol, which waxeth old and shall perish in Babylon, even Babylon the great, which shall fall.

"Wherefore, I the Lord, knowing the calamity which should come upon the inhabitants of the earth, called upon my servant Joseph Smith, Jun., and spake unto him from heaven, and gave him commandments;" - D&C 1:15-17
